E3FA: Horizontally flips the 24 pixels in E,C,B and counterpart masks in E',C',B'.
Used by the routine at plot_masked_sprite_24px.
Input
B Left 8 pixels.
C Middle 8 pixels.
E Right 8 pixels.
Output
B Flipped left 8 pixels.
C Flipped middle 8 pixels.
E Flipped right 8 pixels.
Horizontally flip the bitmap bytes by looking up each byte in the flipped / bit-reversed table at $7F00 and swapping the left and right pixels over.
