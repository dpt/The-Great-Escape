B89C: Get next drawable
This finds the next vischar or item that should be drawn.
Used by the routine at plot_sprites.
Output

A Returns (vischars_LENGTH - iters) if vischar, or ((item__LIMIT - iters) | (1 << 6)) if itemstruct.
